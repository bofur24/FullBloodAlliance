Membership Advantages

The People

You are united with Fullblood Limousin breeders from around the globe, all of them with a strong belief in Fullblood Limousin cattle. When any member talks the benefits of our breed, we all reap the reqrds!

The Advertising

When our alliance advertises every member gains the benefit. The ads direct the interested party to our website where every member is listed.

The website it is like having your own webpage. Breeders looking for Fullblood Limousin go to "fullbloodlimousinalliance.org" first. There they will find your farm/ranch listed in alphabetical order.

The Classifieds for a nominal fee, you can list your animals for sale on our website where they will be viewed by everyone logging on. This puts your ad where it can do the most good, in front of the people looking for your type of cattle!

The Promotion

The many benefits of Fullblood Limousin cattle are highlighted at Fairs and Shows around the country, increasing the demand for and the value of your cattle. Informed stockmen from your area looking for Fullbloods will be directed to you by the personnel working the booth.

Membership Application


You may fill out your membership online through our form below, or you may print the form and mail it in. (Click the buttion above to download and print)

Membership dues for the fullblood Limousin Alliance are $50 a year per farm and can be paid online for your convenience.
