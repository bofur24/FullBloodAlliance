Dear fellow members,
I am sure most of you are busy with winter and haven't even though about the fact that all your dues are being accepted at the many Associations that you might be members of. Everyone appreciates your support, as each of us benefit by the participation of all. The FLA spent almost $5,000.00 in advertising alone last year, which would have not been possible without your $50.00 dues.

Please consider dropping a check in the mail today so we can continue to plan an amazing year promoting your Fullblood Limousin.

Thank you
