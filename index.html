Newsletter

Sept

Hello FLA Members!

Our next meeting will be held in Bowling Green, Kentucky on Oct. 4th, 2019. The board meeting is at 3:00 PM, with the general meeting to follow at 4:00 PM. It should be a great time to visit, and see quality cattle.

There will be a silent auction for the junior program in conjunction with the Foundation Sale. If you would like to make a donation for the auction please bring your items with you to the sale.

Accommodations are available at Super 8, 110 Williametter Lane, Bowling Green, KY 42104. Phone: 270-781-4900.
Also, at Microtel Inn and Suites at 1980 Mel Browning Road, Bowling Green,m KY 42104. Phone: 270-745-9922.
Ask for a discount in conjunction with the Fundation Sale.

Nominations for President, Vice President, Treasure, and Secretary and two board members are due by the end of our next general meeting. Ty Heavin and Stacy Kitchens' positions are up for election. Nominations can be presented at the next meeting, or given to a board member, or emailed to me. Nominations will cease at the end of our next meeting. I would like to step down as secretary, so if you know of someone who is willing to do the job, please make the nomination.

We are looking for places to have our 2020 FLA National Show. We are looking for a venue near or in Oklahoma. Some board members are looking into places and will make a presentation on those places during the next meeting. We will then vote and decide where to have the next show. If you have an idea and would like t opresent that idea, including possible costs, and availability we would love for you to do so!

Conference Call number is 916-558-7514 (cost is .17¢). Pin number is 731187. Please note *6 is self mute.

If you would like something to be added to the agenda of the next meeting, please let a board nember know!

Kim Kosmicke,
Secretary FLA
